Automate and streamline your organisations’ tasks and processes

with Microsoft Power Automate

Optimise Business Processes Through Digital And Robotic Process Automation With Power Automate.

We assist our customers to automate their monotonous, repetitious, and time-absorbing tasks so that they concentrate their energy and knowledge on higher-value tasks and what they are best at.

Previously known "Microsoft Flow", Power Automate as a part of the Power Platform is a service offered by Microsoft to assist organisations to swiftly and effortlessly build automated workflows across their digital ecosystem. Users can create rational workflows between their applications and services to make operations much more productive

Microsoft Power Automate

Boost productivity

Create efficient workflows for automation of admin complicated tasks and integration with third-party apps.

Build secure workflows fast

Utilise secure, based on cloud data loss prevention, identification, and access management service

Build on intelligence

Reap from the inbuilt AI technology, and enables you to concentrate on critical, and value-added opportunities.

Improve Productivity

Enable users to save valuable time from limited tasks through largescale solutions connecting up your business applications and systems. Power automate gives you access to more than 390 connectors that allow you to connect your data, your team and processes.

Quickly and Securely Scale

Users can build quick and without code workflows that can be revised and improved as your organization grows and progress. Power Automate provides On-cloud data loss prevention, Identity along with access management services.

Workflows work for you

Power Automate has built-in AI capabilities that can be applied to workflows, Applying those AI capabilities allow you to automate time-absorbing, manual ,and low-level tasks so that you will be able to focus on higher-value tasks. Moreover, AI builder applies intelligent machine learning capabilities to your flows from the process of text recognition to the generation of automatic forms.
